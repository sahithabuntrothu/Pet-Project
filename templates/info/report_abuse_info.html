Report Pet Abuse

If you suspect a listing on Pet Portal is fake, a scam, or involves animal abuse, we need to know immediately. Our moderation team reviews all reports.

Emergency? If you are witnessing active animal cruelty or an emergency, please call your local authorities or Animal Control immediately.

How to Report a Profile

1
Navigate to the Pet Profile

Go to the specific pet page that you suspect is fraudulent or abusive.

2
Click "Report Fake/Abuse"

You will find the report button at the bottom of the pet's details next to the post timestamp. Note: You must be logged in to report a profile.

3
Provide Clear Details

In the popup form, clearly state why you are reporting the profile (e.g., "The phone number redirects to a scam line," or "The photo is heavily watermarked from another site").

Need Further Assistance?

Contact our support team directly at support@petportal.com.
