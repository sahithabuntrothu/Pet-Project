Lost Pet Checklist

A step-by-step guide on what to do immediately when your pet goes missing.

Search Your Immediate Area Thoroughly

Check all hiding spots in your house, garage, yard, and immediate neighborhood. Cats often hide in small spaces very close to home.

Report on Pet Portal & Social Media

Create a "Lost Pet" profile here with recent, clear photos. Share the link to local Facebook groups, Nextdoor, and neighborhood apps.

Contact Local Shelters & Vets

Call and visit the nearest animal control, humane society, and veterinary clinics. Provide them with a flyer.

Leave Scent Articles Outside

Place their bedding, favorite toys, or a worn piece of your clothing outside to help guide them back by scent.
