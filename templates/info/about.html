About Pet Portal

Pet Portal is a community-driven initiative dedicated to reuniting lost pets with their families and finding loving homes for abandoned animals. Our mission is to create a centralized, easy-to-use platform where every second counts.

Founded in 2026, we have partnered with local shelters, veterinary clinics, and a network of passionate volunteers. Over the years, we have helped thousands of pets find their way back home. Thank you for being part of our journey to make the world a safer place for all animals.

Our Vision

A world where no pet is left behind, and every animal has a safe, loving environment.
