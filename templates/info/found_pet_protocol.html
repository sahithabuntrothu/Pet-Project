Found Pet Protocol

Thank you for helping! Follow these steps to safely reunite the pet with its owner.

Secure the Pet Safely

Approach cautiously. If safe, secure the pet in a fenced area, leash them, or use a carrier. Provide fresh water but avoid large amounts of food initially.

Check for ID Tags

Look for a collar with an ID tag or a rabies tag. If you find a phone number, attempt to call the owner immediately.

Scan for a Microchip

Take the pet to the nearest veterinary clinic or animal shelter to be scanned for a microchip for free.

Create a "Found" Listing

Report the found pet on Pet Portal. Withhold one identifying feature (like a specific marking) so the true owner can verify it when they claim the pet.
